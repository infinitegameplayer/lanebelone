He's doing some amazing things and he shared with me a document of his vision and other aspects of his message.</p><p>Upon glancing at the document, I noticed that more clarity could be articulated with his Vision and no specific mission was shared.</p><p>I have helped many create and refine a Vision and Mission for themselves and their business.</p><p>I offered to help him and right after, I realized that I didn't have one for myself yet lol.</p><p>So I set the intention for my Vision and Mission to reveal itself to me and then let it go.</p><p>At about 4am on Saturday, 26 November 2022, I woke up from a dream full of energy and just wrote down my Vision. I waited a day for any updates to come to me.</p><p>What I wrote down was the following:</p><p><em><strong>"To harmonize pioneering and emerging energies, technologies and paradigms to realize a joyful existence."</strong></em></p><p>I didn't make any changes at all to the Vision.</p><p>An inner voice told me to just share it with the world on Sunday, 27 November 2022.</p><p>A part of me felt I should wait until it was "complete" or "more ready", but the inner voice from my Heart told me to take imperfect action.</p><p>I think it's beautiful that as I reached out to help someone else, Life reminded me of what I needed and I listened.</p><p>My Vision may change a little bit, or a lotta bit in the future. But that's okay. I'm rolling with what I have and moving forward.</p><p>As my journey unfolds, my Vision will be realized and experienced with more clarity and joy.</p><p>I am excited!</p><p><a href="https://lanebelone.com/blog/f/lane-belone-mission" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Click here to read the Mission.</a></p>
