Doesn’t work to actually resolve the problem.</p><p>So what is the last step?</p><p>A precise trajectory forward.</p><p>And I don’t mean perfect by any means.</p><p>A precise trajectory is shifting the focus, attention and energy towards what we really want, rather than being against the old thing.</p><p>This is truly the last step.</p><p>Because it makes the old story non-relevant.</p><p>This is important.</p><p>Remember when the bully calls the other kid a name, but the moment it doesn’t bother the kid, the power of the bully is defused. The bully no longer has any power over the kid.</p><p>This answer is simple, yet it works on every level. &nbsp;Think about it.</p><p>So what is the precise trajectory I’m talking about?</p><p>A trajectory of Joyful Sovereignty.</p><p>And why do I like to use the term trajectory?</p><p>A trajectory indicates a path, course forward or aim; yet it doesn’t have a specific milestone to get caught up in.</p><p>Often times, we get distracted of “getting there”, when we’re really just trying to “get here”.</p><p>You know, the Present Moment.</p><p>So trajectory removes the “goal-ness” that can take us away from Being Present.</p><p>So Joyful Sovereignty.</p><p>That’s my stance. That’s my aim. That’s my trajectory.</p><p>So…</p><p>Who’s coming with me man?! (To quote a specific movie…)</p><p>I’m no military general, but I will lead an experience towards Joyful Sovereignty.</p><p>It will be powerful.</p><p>It will be co-creative.</p><p>It will be life-changing.</p><p>If you’re interested in joining this experience, check out this link.</p><p><a href="https://lanebelone.com/joyful-sovereignty" rel="noopener" target="_blank">https://lanebelone.com/joyful-sovereignty</a></p><p>There’s much more context and content to this, but this message serves as an introduction.</p><p>Happy to converse and connect on this.</p><p>What would be your “precise trajectory” for yourself in the coming years?</p><p>I’d love to hear it.</p>
