<p><a href="https://lanebelone.com/joyful-sovereignty" target="_blank" rel="noopener"></a></p><figure><div><a href="https://lanebelone.com/joyful-sovereignty" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img src="//img1.wsimg.com/isteam/ip/63b44413-1bd1-41f7-a1a3-d748eba816d5/Lane%20Belone%20-%202%20of%203.jpeg/:/cr=t:0%25,l:0%25,w:100%25,h:100%25/rs=w:1280" alt="Dance of Life" title="Dance of Life"><figcaption>Dance of Life</figcaption></a></div></figure><p></p><p>There is a dance to life.</p><p>Many are stuck in the same old dance they were born into.</p><p>A dance of suffering and frustration.</p><p>A dance of never quite getting what they really want.</p><p>A dance of never being quite good enough.</p><p>It can often be a dance that goes with the same song.</p><p>A cyclical existence. A dance and song on loop.</p><p>It’s only when we have the courage to look at the complete opposite of how we’ve looked at life, does something profound happen.</p><p>We see this duality, allowing for contrast.</p><p>This contrast can liberate us, or it can give us more of how we are limited.</p><p>It’s all how you look at it.</p><p>Let’s get practical.</p><p>If you’ve experience highs and lows, good and bad, easy and challenging; you’re living full spectrum. And the more extreme, the more profound.</p><p>A warrior who has found peace is an example.</p><p>A homeless person who becomes a billionaire is another example.</p><p>The lower the times, the sweeter the victory.</p><p>This is the full spectrum. And we all have the ability to dance this full spectrum.</p><p>We are in an age where a precise trajectory will be most advantageous in the coming decade especially.</p><p>This trajectory will guide individuals to live in the Present Moment, trust themselves (especially in uncertain times) and continue their journey beautifully and powerfully.</p><p>And this trajectory will allow any individual to dance with life itself, each and every day, in its full glory.</p><p><a href="https://lanebelone.com/blog/f/a-precise-trajectory-is-the-answer" rel="noopener" target="_blank">This trajectory I will share in article 3 of 3</a>.</p>
