No in between.</p><p>“I’m either Joyful, or I’m not.”</p><p>“I’m either Sovereign, or I’m not.”</p><p>This black and white thinking makes it easy to live in worry, stress and judgement.</p><p>Yes, there are extremes on this spectrum.</p><p>Ultimate joy and depressive lethargy.</p><p>Ultimate sovereignty and controlled oppression.</p><p>Almost all of us are somewhere in the middle.</p><p>But here’s the thing. Even though there is this full spectrum, most only welcome the “good” side of the spectrum.</p><p>The inspiring, the wonderful, the amazing, the incredible, the synchronistic, the good stuff.</p><p>Those are all great things we want in life. Yet, it’s only half of life.</p><p>There is another side of the spectrum that is often neglected.</p><p>The challenging, the heavy, the dense, the hard, the difficult.</p><p>Most avoid this side.</p><p>Most neglect the wisdom of this side.</p><p>Many are adamantly against this side.</p><p>This side brings about a powerful tool for you, contrast.</p><p>How do you know what resonates with you or not? Often times the feeling of “that don’t feel right”. It’s the contrast.</p><p>It’s the opposite of what you want.</p><p>Every have a leader who you know you don’t want to be like?</p><p>They are the contrast to point you in the right direction FOR YOU. No judgement on them, their expressed leadership that doesn’t resonate with you is a gift.</p><p>An absolute gift.</p><p>For those who half-heartedly embrace this contrasting side, only do so to a degree. Often it’s only embraced on the mental aspect.</p><p>Trust me, I’ve been there. And I continue to move that “knowing” from my head to my heart.</p><p>Accepting there is a spectrum and embracing the wisdom of all of it. That is the journey of enjoying the fullness of life.</p><p>Moving forward in life now, you see the pain, triggers, friction and suffering as your direct path to your own liberation.</p><p>Let’s look at something.</p><p></p><figure><div><img src="//img1.wsimg.com/isteam/ip/63b44413-1bd1-41f7-a1a3-d748eba816d5/Joy%201.PNG/:/cr=t:0%25,l:0%25,w:100%25,h:100%25/rs=w:1280"></div></figure><p></p><p>Here is a spectrum of Joy. There are many aspects that give deep meaning for all of us as humans.</p><p>The ability to utilize our free will to decide our <em>intentions</em> and where we place our <em>attention</em>.</p><p>The feeling of having agency in our own lives. The “knowing” that We The People can choose our own fate in each moment.</p><p>There is also a shadow side of it too.</p><p>Many can go overly against the current “system” and gain “extreme” freedom.</p><p>Perhaps that’s their journey, yet when you go against something, you keep it alive to a degree.</p><p>In later editions of the Joyful Sovereignty Weekly, we will dive deeper into how powerful our spoken word is and how it creates our life as we speak.</p><p>Let’s look at another spectrum.</p><p></p><figure><div><img src="//img1.wsimg.com/isteam/ip/63b44413-1bd1-41f7-a1a3-d748eba816d5/Soveriegnty.PNG/:/cr=t:0%25,l:0%25,w:100%25,h:100%25/rs=w:1280"></div></figure><p></p><p>Where do you land on this spectrum of feeling Sovereignty in your daily life?</p><p>Again, let’s use “Person A” as an example. When they truly sit with themselves and their life, they actually feel they land on a -2.</p><p>They actually feel sad that they don’t find themselves higher on this spectrum.</p><p>That’s totally okay, because everyone is exactly where they are.</p><p>The point of this is to honestly assess where you’re at. No judgement.</p><p>Only when we’re honest with ourselves and where we’re at, can we make some significant moves forward.</p><p>With all the aspects of Sovereignty, we will focus on a Sovereign mind and body.</p><p>To free your mind of programming and to liberate your body to expand the capacity to hold more joy and sovereignty in a sustainable and continuous way.</p><p>Speaking of the body, it is the holder for our next insight.</p><p><br></p><div><h2>Joyful Sovereignty is a Feeling</h2></div><p>As much as we want to understand Joyful Sovereignty from the mental perspective, the deeper truth is that it’s actually a feeling.</p><p>The mind can try to describe it, but the feeling will always be foundational.</p><p>Feelings occur IN the body.</p><p>Our understanding helps us integrate the mind and body to exist in harmony.</p><p>Moving forward on this journey, we are harmonizing the mental intention of becoming more joyful and more sovereign, with the feeling of joyfulness and sovereignty in the body.</p><p>We can practice and teach ourselves to have the feelings of Joyfulness and Sovereignty. This happens by directing our attention and awareness on the Present Moment.</p><p>Being Present helps the nervous system feel safe.</p><p>We won’t go too deep into the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous systems, but essentially, we are tapping into the parasympathetic nervous system to calm and relax ourselves.</p><p>Activating your parasympathetic nervous system sends blood and resources to our recovery systems of our body&nbsp;like reproduction, immunity, sleep and digestion.</p><p>Becoming fully seated into joy and sovereignty is a byproduct.</p><p>One simple way to tap into the healing effects of the parasympathetic nervous system is to take a deep breath in for 4 seconds, hold your breath for 4 seconds. Then very slowly breath out for 6 or more seconds.</p><p>As you exhale, loosen your body and release any tension.</p><p>When you practice this, you may notice that your awareness is distributed from one place (such as in your head) to your entire body.</p><p>This is healing because it lessens the load your mind has. Decreasing mental stress and worry.</p><p>From a biological perspective, this puts your body into restoration mode, making you feel safe.</p><p>When our nervous system feels safe, WE feel safe.</p><p>When we feel safe, we can open ourselves up to higher states of being.</p><p>Such as Joyfulness and Sovereignty.</p><p>If this information is completely new to you, don’t worry about understanding it all.</p><p>All of this is meant to plant a seed of understanding how your mind and body are interconnected so deeply. And how it is connected to our journey towards Joyful Sovereignty.</p><p>It is also intended to share some more of the science behind this message.</p><p><br></p><div><h2>Gratitude is the Key</h2></div><p>Instead of placing our attention towards the external world and all the seemingly growing chaos, we direct our focus on something simple.</p><p>Gratitude.</p><p>We do this for the simple reason that we gain control in our lives again.</p><p>We decide what we focus on, what is happening in OUR world and being grateful for it.</p><p>Just by controlling our attention more, we expand our capacity to <em>observe</em> joyfulness and sovereignty in our life, as well as <em>feel it</em> more in our bodies.</p><p>That’s just the start of it, because gratitude is so powerful.</p><p>When you become grateful for the “good stuff” in your life, you get more “good stuff”.</p><p>When you become grateful for the “bad stuff” in your life, you get less “bad stuff”, because you don’t really view it as bad stuff anymore. You realize these experiences are actually useful to you.</p><p>Being grateful for the challenging times is what amplifies your capacity to feel Joyful Sovereignty.</p><p>As we shared in the beginning of this article, most people AREN’T embracing the “bad stuff”.</p><p>Here’s another side effect of being grateful for the hard stuff. You actually can enjoy the “good stuff” on a deeper level.</p><p>Know anyone who can’t seem to celebrate even the huge wins in life? They brush it off as just a regular day.</p><p>This practice of gratitude expands your capacity to feel all of it fully. Yes, the challenging times can feel uncomfortable in the beginning.
