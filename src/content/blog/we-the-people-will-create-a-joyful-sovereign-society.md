Behind you is a land of old stories, old paradigms and old energies. In front of you, is a land of new stories, new paradigms and new energies.</p><p>The bridge you walk across isn’t all that stable, but it’s the route you’ve found yourself navigating.</p><p><br></p><div><h2>The Old World</h2></div><p>There is a part of the world that doesn’t have your best interest at heart.</p><p>The mainstream media, the pharmaceutical industry, the food industry, certain governmental organizations, etc.; <em>most likely</em> don’t want consumers to reach their potential, be joyful or experience freedom to their fullest capacity.</p><p>This isn’t a bash on these industries as a whole, but there are obvious observations that indicate the human potential isn’t at the heart of these industries and organizations.</p><p>Many are saying hard times and big uncertainties are in the near future. Others are saying they are already here.</p><p>People and experts will have their opinions and perspectives. But one thing rings true.</p><p>You can't control the outside world. Yet the outside world can affect you.</p><p>Many believe old systems are beginning to crumble and fall. They may be right, or they may be wrong. In any case, there is a way to navigate life moving forward.</p><p>If the world is changing in ways you can't control, what do you do?</p><p>You take ownership of your journey and trust yourself with each step. No matter if the day looks gloomy or it's the most wonderful day. You trust yourself wholly and fully.</p><p>Life gives you everything you need in this moment, but sometimes it may not look like a gift until you shift your perspective.</p><p>If you’re on that bridge, the Old World is behind us.</p><p><br></p><div><h2><strong>The Problem Fighting Fire With Fire</strong></h2></div><p>When we look at the Old World, it can be easy to try to fight against it. We think that by “defeating” the old ways, we will win. There’s something subtle that happens when we fight.</p><p><strong>Fighting against an old story still feeds into that story.</strong></p><p>It brings relevance to that old story and keeps it alive. If we truly want to let go of the old story and old ways of life, we need to stop feeding it our energy and attention.</p><p>This not only works for you in your personal life but also for the world at large.</p><p>Remember, we’re on an old wooden bridge, would fire be the best tool for us?</p><p><br></p><div><h2>The Joyful Sovereignty Trajectory</h2></div><p>Perhaps the best “tool” we have is our feet. To move forward on our Path.</p><p>To keep going, towards a specific trajectory.</p><p>What you place your energy and attention on, get amplified. So instead of "fighting" your old story, you simply shift your focus to your new story.</p><p>Yes, that’s easier said than done.</p><p>That's what this trajectory is all about, supporting you on your path. We will help each other focus on the way forward, not get caught up on the old story of the past.</p><p><strong>We will hold each other accountable and continue to take the next step powerfully and boldly.</strong></p><p>Let's face it, the last several years have taught us to Be more authentic and not hold ourselves back anymore. This shared and precise trajectory will be incredibly powerful and will help you reveal your true nature.</p><p>The byproduct of not fighting and not focusing on the Old Stories is they become non-relevant in your life more and more each day.</p><p>To be sure, we aren’t turning a blind eye to what is happening in the world. We are simply becoming more intentional about where we focus our attention and energy.</p><p>The Joyful Sovereignty Trajectory is the process.
