<p></p><figure><div><img src="//img1.wsimg.com/isteam/ip/63b44413-1bd1-41f7-a1a3-d748eba816d5/Archetypal%20Expressions%20-%20Harmonious%20Creator.png/:/cr=t:0%25,l:0%25,w:100%25,h:100%25/rs=w:1280"></div></figure><p></p><p>Have you ever laughed so hard at yourself after doing something so frustrating?</p><p>I remember this one time I was recording a video with a positive message over and over. I kept messing up the words, not sounding like I wanted to and forgetting some of the message.</p><p>Over time, I got really frustrated and even angry at myself because I couldn’t get it right.</p><p>Finally, after 32 trys I got it good enough that I could share. Then all of a sudden, I started laughing at how ridiculous I was.</p><p>Here I was, sharing a positive message but feeling so frustrated at the moment.</p><p>Eventually, I’d come to appreciate this moment and other frustrating moments.</p><p>But it wasn’t until I had some time and distance from the emotionally charged moment that I could make light of it.</p><p>Here’s an obvious approach to life.</p><p>Have appreciation for all of your creation, even the challenging stuff.</p><p>You are responsible for your life and what is happening for you. On a certain level, you set the wheels in motion to experience what you’re experiencing right now.</p><p>Here’s an interesting perspective.</p><p>During challenging times, you are intentionally allowing frustration, friction, misalignment to be empowered and expressed.</p><p>But why would you do that?</p><p>That’s a great good question.</p><p>And the beauty of this question is that you’re asking from a state of empowerment. Which will lead to an empowered answer.</p><p>An empowered answer you do can do something with.</p><p>You may not believe this, so perhaps you can try it out.</p><p>Here are 5 simple steps to experience it for yourself.</p><p></p><ol>
  <li>Identify the challenge you’re navigating right now that’s giving you massive frustration</li>
  <li>Ask yourself: “If I set this experience in motion, what would be behind that?”</li>
  <li>Answer honestly</li>
  <li>Decide on one single thing to accept from this situation</li>
  <li>Thank this aspect of you for expressing itself and showing you wisdom</li>
</ol><p></p><p>Sounds simple. And life can be simple, if you welcome it.</p><p>And you can also transmute frustration into liberation.</p><p>What part resonated with you most? Do you have your own example?</p><p>Btw, you can still see the video on <a href="https://www.instagram.com/increasefreedom/" rel="">my Instagram</a> from January 2017. See if you can guess which one that video is lol.</p>
