wake up at 4-something AM, brimming with ideas and energy of Creation.</p><p>I jot what needs to be jotted.</p><p>Organize ideas as it makes sense.</p><p>Plot action in my calendar for those ideas that feel timely.</p><p>The idea of “what will 2023 look like” has surfaced the past couple days.</p><p>One morning, with the Sun still napping and darkness surrounding, a tiny subtle thought form floated into my head.</p><p>That if I had all the power in the world to experience or manifest anything, I’d want everyone around me to get what their heart desires.</p><p>They would get lucky.</p><p>They would receive the good news.</p><p>They would realize causeless joy.</p><p>They would let go of an old story that once gripped their lives.</p><p>They would get the dream job.</p><p>They would find clarity in their purpose.</p><p>They would spark courage to take action on their dreams.</p><p><br></p><p>All the while, I’d get texts, messages and calls from all these people I so deeply care about, hearing of their beautiful experiences.</p><p>Through their miraculous messages, I receive such deep joy and appreciation for life.</p><p>Sparking such an open Heart in me during the process.</p><p>There’s an even deeper story here, but that’s for another time.</p><p>To say this might feel “in the clouds”, can be an easy perspective to subscribe to.</p><p>All depending on how you read this and what thoughts floated in your mind in between these sentences.</p><p>Or perhaps you deeply resonated with some of these words.</p><p>Either way, this is expressed from an intentionally optimistic, yet Earthy and grounded essence.</p><p>Of course, this isn’t the only thing I’m manifesting in 2023, but it’s a good one I am enjoying the anticipation of.</p><p>I created a manifestation meditation recently and not sure if people would be interested in sampling it.</p><p>If there’s enough interest, I can share it.</p><p>Wish you a wonderful day!</p>
